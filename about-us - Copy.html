About Us

  

NewsNG.com is an automated online News portal that regularly pulls news articles from various Nigerian newspapers and media, collating these different sorces into one central location for visitors to read and collect.

What makes NewsNG unique?
Our Ranking System (Buzz): Ideally stories are ranked in order of importance by the Editors of each media platform. However, people might find the Editors' choices boring or biased. With www.NewsNG.com, we are seeking to correct this using Social merdia as a basis for our ranking. So after collecting news from different sources, NewsNG takes each article to the major Social Media platforms to find out how many people are sharing a story on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Google+ and ranks all these different stories based on how popular they are online, in essence, effectively transforming the readers to editors.
